# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 24, 2026

**App:** Skedoff
**Developer:** Cahyanudien Aziz Saputra
**Company:** PT FlagoDNA Developer Labs

---

## Overview

Skedoff is built on one principle: your content is yours. The app works entirely offline. No account is required. No data ever leaves your device.

---

## What We Collect

**Nothing.**

Skedoff does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or usage data — not now, not ever.

---

## How Your Data Is Stored

All data created in Skedoff — your drafts, queued posts, published records, platform tags, and settings — is stored locally on your device using an on-device database (Isar). This data:

- Never leaves your device
- Is never transmitted to any server
- Is never backed up to any cloud service
- Is removed completely when you uninstall the app

---

## Third-Party Services

Skedoff integrates with no third-party services of any kind. There are no:

- Analytics SDKs
- Advertising networks
- Crash reporting tools
- Cloud storage services
- Social media SDKs
- Data brokers or trackers

---

## Permissions

Skedoff may request the following permissions solely for core app functionality:

| Permission               | Purpose                                                            | Data Transmitted? |
| ------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------ | ----------------- |
| `INTERNET`               | Required by the Flutter framework                                  | No                |
| `ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE`   | Connectivity status display only                                   | No                |
| `RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED` | Restore local notification reminders after reboot (future version) | No                |
| `POST_NOTIFICATIONS`     | Local reminder notifications (future version)                      | No                |

No permission is used to collect or transmit data.

---

## Notifications

When enabled in a future version, all notifications in Skedoff are **local only** — generated on your device, not from any external server or service. No content from your drafts is transmitted in order to deliver a notification.

---

## Advertising

Skedoff is completely ad-free. There are no advertisements, no ad SDKs, and no advertising identifiers used anywhere in the app.

---

## Analytics

Skedoff has no analytics. Your usage patterns, writing habits, posting schedules, and interactions with the app are never observed or recorded.

---

## Children's Privacy

Skedoff does not collect information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

---

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the new version will be published here with a revised effective date. Since no data is collected, changes will only ever reflect new features — not new data practices.

---

## Your Rights

You have full control over your data at all times:

- Edit or delete any draft directly within the app
- Export your data as `.db` at any time
- Uninstall the app to remove all data from your device permanently

No data exists on any external server to request, transfer, or delete.
