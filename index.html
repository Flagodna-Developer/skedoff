Search across all drafts instantly. Filter by platform, status, or date range to find what you need fast.

Your data

Nothing leaves your device. Ever.

Most content tools make money by storing your content. Skedoff is different. There is no server. There is no sync. Your drafts are stored locally using the Isar database, on your phone, in your hands.

  • Zero analytics or telemetry
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data
  • No network requests from the app
  • Local-only storage — nothing is ever uploaded

Meet Skedoff
Workstation.

Prefer a bigger screen? Skedoff Workstation is the desktop companion that lets you edit your phone drafts directly from your PC — full keyboard, full mouse, zero cloud.

Connect over local Wi-Fi in seconds. Changes save instantly back to your phone. No accounts, no syncing to a third-party server, no subscription. Just you, your keyboard, and your drafts.

Connect instantly via local Wi-Fi

No cables, no Bluetooth pairing. Just open Workstation on your PC and it finds your phone automatically on the same network.

Changes save instantly to your phone

Every edit you make on your PC syncs back to your phone in real-time. No manual save buttons. No "sync now" needed.

No cloud, no accounts, no data leaving your network

Everything stays on your local Wi-Fi. Zero data goes to any server. No sign-up, no login, no tracking. Complete privacy.
